The ISO/TC 211 Harmonized Resources Maintenance Agency (HRMA) is pleased
to announce the launch of a redesigned
https://www.isotc211.org[www.isotc211.org] resource site.

The redesign delivers a modern, mobile-first experience for accessing
ISO/TC 211 harmonized resources — including UML models, XML schemas,
ontologies, registries, and terminology — with improved navigation,
responsive layouts, and dark mode support.

== Incremental rollout

This is the first step in an incremental rollout across all ISO/TC 211
resource sites. The next site to be updated will be the
https://schemas.isotc211.org[schemas.isotc211.org] XML schema repository,
followed by other HRMA-managed properties in due course.

By rolling out changes incrementally, we can gather feedback from the
community and ensure each site meets the needs of its users before moving
on to the next.

== What's new

* **Modern design** — clean layout with clear visual hierarchy and
  improved readability across all screen sizes.
* **Responsive** — fully mobile-first, adapting gracefully from phones
  to wide desktops.
* **Dark mode** — automatic detection of system preference with manual
  toggle, persisted across sessions.
* **Improved resource access** — resources are presented in a card-based
  grid with direct links to each service.
* **Open source** — the site is developed in the open on
  https://github.com/ISO-TC211[GitHub], and contributions are welcome.

== About the HRMA

The HRMA was established under
https://committee.iso.org/sites/tc211/home/resolutions.html[ISO/TC 211 CIB Resolution 2021-16]
and is operated by the https://www.sis.se[Swedish Institute of Standards (SIS)],
with hosting and platform support provided by https://www.ribose.com[Ribose].
It governs all ISO/TC 211 harmonized resources published at isotc211.org.

We welcome feedback on the new site. Please open an issue on the
https://github.com/ISO-TC211/www.isotc211.org[site repository] or reach
out through the usual ISO/TC 211 channels.
